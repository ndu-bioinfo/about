About Me

Computational Biologist & Bioinformatics Scientist

Welcome to my personal website! I'm a computational biologist and bioinformatics scientist with a passion for integrating computational strategies into biological research.

During my PhD, I dove deep into researching pathways associated with high light stress in a marine diatom by applying fluxomic and transcriptomic data. This journey had me spending countless hours in the wet lab, collecting and analyzing data to build accurate computational models.

In my professional career as a postdoctoral researcher and industry scientist, I've dedicated much of my time to collaborating with sequencing teams to build and optimize pipelines tailored to research needs. I ensure quality assurance policies are effectively implemented by integrating QC checks into these pipelines. Working closely with wet lab teams, I've developed custom tools to enhance workflow efficiency and troubleshoot issues. My role also involves data management, including building and maintaining databases and creating APIs for data querying and visualization. Notably, I built two production-level pipelines that laid the foundation for on/off-target analysis and risk assessment in a major project.
