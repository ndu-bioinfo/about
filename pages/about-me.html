About Me

Bioinformatics Researcher & Data Scientist

I am a bioinformatics researcher and data scientist with expertise in genomics and computational biology. My work spans from marine biology to human health, focusing on developing and optimizing data analysis pipelines for complex biological systems.

Key areas of experience include Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS), immune response mapping, genetic diagnostics, and drug discovery for rare diseases. I specialize in applying machine learning and Bayesian models to extract meaningful insights from large-scale biological datasets.

My passion lies in leveraging data science to unlock the mysteries of human health and contribute to the advancement of medical treatments.
